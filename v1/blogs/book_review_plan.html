Book Reviews

Hopefully I'll be writing something about what I read.

I have three goals for these book reviews. The first is thinking about older books in the context of modern life. The second is thinking about communication and how authors build the message of their books and create the impressions that the reader is left with. Lastly, I want to spend some time thinking about books and sort of "social theory" in the context of my meditation practice. I think it would be interesting to interpret books through the lens that buddhism views the world the way that people intrepret books according to Marxism or gender theory.

Each book review will answer a few basic questions, do I recommend the book and/or why might someone be interested in reading this book. What is the books overall message or impression that the reader is left with. How does the author create this message or impression. How does that methodology and the plot relate to the way that buddhism interprets the world.
